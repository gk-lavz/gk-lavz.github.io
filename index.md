# How I Spent My 1st Special Leave of 2026

**June 17th** marked our **4th Wedding Anniversary**. 

Before diving into how we celebrated, here is a little background about my wife: she is a veterinary doctor and two years younger than me. Interestingly, she initially picked engineering at the exact same college and department where I studied. However, God had other plans for her—she dropped out of engineering after two months and later pursued veterinary science instead!

---

### Ticking Off the Bucket List

In these four years together, we have enjoyed a lot of great food, both home-cooked and from our favorite spots outside. But there was always one specific dish on our culinary bucket list that we just couldn't seem to tick off for some reason: **Mutton Kola Urandai** (traditional spiced meat balls).

I decided that this anniversary was the perfect time to change that. 

```
[ Culinary Bucket List ]
[x] Mutton Kola Urandai (Home-cooked!)
```

Armed with a few YouTube tutorial videos, I stepped into the kitchen and prepared them from scratch at home. We ate them together that afternoon. To my absolute delight, she thoroughly enjoyed them—in fact, she liked the homemade *Kola Urandai* even more than her anniversary gift!

---

### Looking Ahead

It was a day incredibly well spent, and we finally crossed a long-standing item off our bucket list. Hoping for many more milestones, experiments, and delicious meals to come...
